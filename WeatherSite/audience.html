Target Audience

My websites target audience is designed for people who love to look at the weather to prepare for the week.

Persona

  • Jim runs a hotdog stand on Ocean Avenue. Jim uses Poprocks weather to determine how long he will keep his hotdog stand open through the week.
  • Sarah is a online marketer who loves to practice her fishing whenever she can. She uses Pop Rocks Weather to balance what hours she can put into fishing so she can efficiently balance her online sales.
